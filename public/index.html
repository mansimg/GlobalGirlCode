Welcome to GlobalGirlCode!

We are a nonprofit organization in the Bay Area working to build a community of high school girls who are interested in coding and computer science. If interested fill out the form below!

Who are we?

We are a group of high school students who seek to close the gender gap in CS by inspiring middle school girls to code early. This helps spark their interest in coding and technology.

What do we do?

Hackathons, workshops, tours and guest speakers!
